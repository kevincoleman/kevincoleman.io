Four Feathered Sparrow: A Salon Website

The website for Four Feathered Sparrow was one of my first business sites. I initially started it out with potential to scale. Over time I learned that it’s a simple, static site—so I scaled it back to be just that. Sometimes it’s easy to let projects get big and bloated because of their perceived potential. This site is a reminder to me to keep projects in the present.

This site has continued to serve the client’s needs, and only occasionally does it need content maintenance. You can visit the site at fourfeatheredsparrow.com.
